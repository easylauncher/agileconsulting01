Term Life Insurance  

Individual Term Life Insurance is one of the simplest ways to protect your family from an untimely death or severe illness during your life time without eroding your hard earned wealth. It is one of the important tools of financial planning.

Life Insurance costs invariably increase with age and the less healthy someone is, the premiums are higher. In a Term Life Insurance Policy the cost of premium is locked for the duration of term. Therefore, the recommendation is to get the most insurance you can comfortably afford, while still young and health. You can always get additional policies at a later point in time if your financial sitation improves. Know that all the policies stack up and the death benefit / living benefits can be stacked up and can be claimed if a qualifying event occurs. 

Permanent Life Insurance

Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific term, with lower premiums and no cash value, while permanent life insurance offers lifelong coverage, higher premiums, cash value accumulation, and potential investment benefits.

Annuities

During the accumulation phase of annuities, individuals can benefit from tax-deferred growth, capital preservation, and asset diversification options, which help secure their savings and prepare for a steady income stream in retirement.
